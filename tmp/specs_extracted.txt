Budo League App
Living Product Specification (Source of Truth)
Version v0.11 • Snapshot date: 18 Dec 2025
1. Match Detail Screen (Post-match) — MVP
Match Detail is the main destination from Home 'Match Centre' and from Results cards. MVP is 
post-match first.
1.1 Header
Top bar: back arrow (left), title 'Match Centre' (center), share icon (right, optional).
1.2 Scoreboard hero section (top of page)
Teams row: Home team (left) vs Away team (right).
Large score in the middle: e.g., 2–1.
Status chip: FT.
Meta line: Sunday • 2:00 PM • Pitch A.
1.3 Key actions (optional MVP)
Buttons/links: 'Lineups' (optional), 'Stats' (optional), 'Report' (default).
For MVP, default to Report tab content.
1.4 Content blocks (MVP)
Match Report: simple paragraph summary (admin/editor).
Timeline (optional for MVP): key events list (Goal, Yellow, Red) with minute; can be added 
later.
Man of the Match (optional): player name + house/team.
1.5 Footer links
Link: 'Back to Results'
Link: 'View Table'
2. Post-match First Rule (locked)
Match Detail prioritizes FT view and report content; live minute-by-minute is deferred.
3. League Scheduling (locked)
All matches are played on Sunday.
Venues: Pitch A and Pitch B.
